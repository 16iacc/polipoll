About

Politicians often use their positions to amass wealth at the cost of quality public services. It is important that we remain vigilant to ensure that this does not happen. But how?

We need to connect the dots between public officials and their private interests. This is where you come in. We need your help to connect the dots.

A person’s name is often spelled differently across different databases. And there are many databases. This makes it difficult to know if a politician is connected to a company. This makes it difficult to connect the dots.

In as little as a few seconds a day you can put YOUR knowledge to work by connecting the dots. If you recognize a connection, match it. It’s fun. It’s easy. Play often.
